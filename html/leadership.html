Christopher Spolar

Christopher and his wife, Jenna, have always called the San Gabriel Valley home. Growing up in a Christian household with five siblings, Christopher’s faith journey truly deepened in college where he felt he began to follow Jesus as a disciple. It was on his college debate team, running a “Jesus ethics” argument, that he first discovered his passion for sharing the transformative way of Jesus with others. Christopher and his wife, Jenna, have always called the San Gabriel Valley home. Growing up in a Christian household with five siblings, Christopher’s faith journey truly deepened in college where he felt he began to follow Jesus as a disciple. It was on his college debate team, running a “Jesus ethics” argument, that he first discovered his passion for sharing the transformative way of Jesus with others. Following a season of deep discernment and prayer, and with the blessing of his former church, Christopher and his family embarked on a new chapter—planting a church in partnership with the Free Methodist Church. Christopher and Jenna live in Alhambra with their three children, excited for the next steps in their faith journey together.
